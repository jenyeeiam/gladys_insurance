  • Day 1

    Travel Day

    You arrive in Vancouver at 4pm, allowing a proper hotel check in with a few hours to explore the area around your hotel. Dinner is at Royal Dinette Downtown.

  • Day 2

    Downtown and Stanley Park

    After breakfast at Jam Cafe you take the bus to Denman St where you rent a bike to explore Stanley Park. In the afternoon, you window shop along Granville Robson Street while stopping for sweets at Thierry Chocolates.

  • Day 3

    Grouse Mtn and Capilano Bridge

    Day 3 is about exploring the local mountains of Vancouver. Take the free shuttle to Grouse Mountain where a gondola will take you to the summit. Eat lunch while overlooking the city.

  • Day 4

    Last day and travel home

    After a successful trip is complete, it's an early wake up for an 8:30am departure from Vancouver back to Phoenix. As you reflect back on your trip, all you can think about is where the next adventure will take you
